Introduction to Korean Universities

Seoul National University

As the top national university in Korea, it leads world-class academics and research. It offers a wide range of majors and broad educational opportunities.

Korea University

It is a prestigious private university in South Korea. With a long history, it nurtures talented individuals based on a liberal and righteous academic tradition.

Yonsei University

It is a private university that boasts a beautiful campus and an international educational environment. It offers various overseas exchange programs and innovative curricula.

Ewha Womans University

As the first higher education institution for women in Korea, it has served as a cradle for nurturing female talent. It produces graduates who are active in various fields of society.

Gachon University

It is a future-oriented university equipped with state-of-the-art facilities. It emphasizes practical studies and provides field-oriented education through industry-academia collaboration.

Ajou University

It is a university focused on fostering global talent. It has outstanding research achievements, especially in the field of engineering, and is also very popular among international students.
